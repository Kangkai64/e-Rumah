USE CASE CONSTRAINTS [C]
Verified against Codebase (Controller, Service, View, Validation) on 2025-12-23

C1. APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY & PERSONAL INFORMATION (Step 1)
---------------------------------------------------------
[Mandatory Fields]
- Name (as per NRIC)
- NRIC No. (Format: xxxxxx-xx-xxxx)
- Date of Birth (Auto-filled from IC, must be reviewed)
- Sex (Auto-filled from IC)
- Malaysian Citizenship (Must be checked/True)
- Marital Status
- Residential Address (Max 60 chars)
- Postcode (5 digits)
- Email (Valid format)
- Residence Phone (Format: xxx-xxxxxxx)
- Mobile Phone (H/P) (Format: xxx-xxxxxxx)
- Occupation
- Employer Name
- Employer Address
- Employer Postcode
- Purpose of Application
- Number of Dependents (Default is '0', but field is mandatory)

[Optional Fields]
- Race
- Dependents' Ages (Only required if Number of Dependents > 0)

[Strict Validation Logic]
- Age Rule: Applicant must be at least 55 years old (calculated from DOB).
- Citizenship Rule: Field must be explicitly checked.
- Phone Rule: Both Residence and Mobile phones are enforced by validation logic, even if UI implies flexibility.

C2. JOINT APPLICANT (Step 2 - Conditional)
------------------------------------------
[Condition]
- Required only if "Do you have a Joint Applicant?" is checked in Step 1.

[Mandatory Fields (If Joint Applicant exists)]
- Salutation
- Name (as per NRIC)
- NRIC No.
- Date of Birth & Sex (Auto-filled)
- Malaysian Citizenship (Must be checked)
- Marital Status
- Relationship to Applicant
- Residential Address & Postcode
- Email
- Residence Phone & Mobile Phone
- Occupation & Employer Details

[Optional Fields]
- Race

[Strict Validation Logic]
- Citizenship Rule: Joint Applicant must also be Malaysian.
- Banking Rule: If Joint Applicant exists, Account Type MUST be "Joint Account Saving" or "Joint Account Current".

C3. BANKING INFORMATION (Step 2)
--------------------------------
[Mandatory Fields]
- Bank Name
- Account Type (Savings / Current / Joint Savings / Joint Current)
- Account Number

C4. PROPERTY INFORMATION (Step 3)
---------------------------------
[Mandatory Fields]
- Property Type
- Property Address
- Postcode
- Indicative Market Value
- Tenure Title (Freehold / Leasehold)
- Property Encumbered (Yes / No)
- Fire Insurance Status (In Force / Not Available)
- Fire Insurance Renewal Option

[Strict Validation Logic]
- Location Rule: Property must be in Kuala Lumpur (Valid postcodes: 41100, 42100, 42000, 45800, 45600, 42500, 42600, 45000, 42700, 43950, 42200, 41300, 41050).
- Encumbrance Rule: Property MUST be free from encumbrances. Selecting "Yes" for "Property Encumbered" triggers a hard blocking error.
- Leasehold Rule: If Tenure is Leasehold, "Expiry Date" is mandatory. Remaining lease must be >= 90 years.

C5. FIRE INSURANCE DETAILS (Step 3 - Conditional)
-------------------------------------------------
[Condition]
- Dependent on "Fire & Home Insurance" selection.

[If "In Force"]
- Insurance Company (Mandatory)
- Period Validity (Mandatory)
- Document: Copy of Policy (Mandatory)

[If "Not Available"]
- Fields disabled. Organization (Cagamas) will purchase policy.

C6. NOMINEE INFORMATION (Step 4)
--------------------------------
[Nominee 1 - Mandatory]
- Name, NRIC, DOB, Sex, Marital Status
- Relationship to Applicant
- Address, Postcode, Email
- Residence Phone & Mobile Phone
- Malaysian Citizenship (Must be checked)
- Race is Optional.

[Nominee 2 - Optional]
- Only required if "Add Second Nominee" checkbox is selected.
- If selected, all Nominee 2 fields become Mandatory (same requirements as Nominee 1).

C7. DOCUMENT CHECKLIST (Step 5)
-------------------------------
[System Generated Requirements]
1. Applicant NRIC (Required)
2. Birth Certificate (Required)
3. Payslips (Latest 3 months - Required)
4. Bank Statements (Latest 6 months - Required)
5. EPF Statement (Required)
6. Grant / Title Deed (Required)
7. Sale & Purchase Agreement (Required)
8. Valuation Report (Required)

[Conditional Documents]
9. Joint Applicant NRIC (Required if Joint Applicant exists)
10. Marriage Certificate (Required if Married + Joint Applicant)
11. Fire Insurance Policy (Required if Insurance is "In Force")
12. Property Loan Statement (Required if Encumbered - though encumbrance blocks submission)

C8. SIGNATURES & DECLARATION (Step 5 & 6)
-----------------------------------------
[Step 5 - Declaration]
- Applicant Signature (Mandatory)
- Joint Applicant Signature (Mandatory if Joint Applicant exists)

[Step 6 - Acknowledgement]
- Nominee Name, NRIC, Address (Auto-filled)
- Nominee Consent Checkbox (Mandatory)
- Nominee Signature (Mandatory)
